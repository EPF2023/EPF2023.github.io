Registration fee is CNY 1500 for students and postdocs and CNY 2000 for faculty members and research staffs. This includes coffee/tea breaks, lunches and dinners during the conference (December 16-19), and the dinner on December 15.

In order to complete the registration, everyone, including the confirmed speakers and pre-registered participants, should fill out the registration form and pay the registration fee.
