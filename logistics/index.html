Conference venue

EPF2023 will be held at Club Med Joyview@Yanqing (Club Med地中海邻境 北京延庆度假村). Club Med Joyview@Yanqing is next to the 4A-level Scenic Spot of Beijing Songshan Natural Reserve (北京松山自然保护区). Its location is shown on the following map, together with the primary airports/railway stations and some popular locations.

For participants from abroad, we highly recommend to take a taxi to get from airports/railway stations to the hotel. For domestic participants, we recommend to directly take a taxi to the hotel or first take the high-speed train from Qinghe/Beijingbei Station to Yanqing Station and then take a taxi to the hotel.

Excursion

Yanqing is a place with many tourist attractions, including the famous Badaling Great Wall. Since the 2022 Beijing Winter Olympics, Yanqing has also been attracting more and more people for its ski resorts.

The afternoon of Day 3 (December 18) is free and we encourage you to explore the Yanqing area. While we will not organize formal excursion trips, we can provide the following two options for people to consider.

Yanqing National Alpine Skiing Centre Badaling Great Wall

Google service

Google service is not available in mainland China without a valid VPN. If your life and work heavily rely on google products (email, map, etc), please identify and set up a good VPN service before arrival. You may also find some local alternatives (e.g., Wechat ,Baidu map, etc) if needed.

Visa information

  4. Gender;
  5. Nationality;
  6. Birth date;
  7. Arrival and departure dates, total duration of your visit;
  8. Other information that you would like to provide (e.g., multiple entry, extended visit, etc).
